Paint / Pavement

A signal economy audit of six systems
draft 0.1
n = 6 systems

Every designed system carries meaning in two economies. Paint is fast: labels, icons, copy, color swatches — cheap to ship, cheap to change, declared above the substrate. Pavement is slow: typography grain, spacing, density, motion timing, material. Baked in, expensive to re-declare, signaling without being read.
