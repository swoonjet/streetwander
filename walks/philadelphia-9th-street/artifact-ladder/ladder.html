FIELD GUIDE · COMPANION · TAXA OF ENCLOSURE

The Ladder of Enclosure

One function — a covered pedestrian zone — at six steps of decreasing mass.
6rungs

The same job, done with less material at each step. Shelter is subtracted; the convention holds. Every rung is a compression of the one above, and every rung still works — until it doesn't. The final stage is probably pure convention, a dashed line on the asphalt or an entry in a mapping app's metadata.

rung 01
1 : 1

The arcade Arcus lapideus

Material
tens of tonnes of dressed stone, engaged piers, semicircular arches
What it carries
weather · sound · the state's permission to pass
Era
Roman to Renaissance

The heaviest ancestor. Every rung below is a reduction of this one — arches kept, mass dropped, at ever finer ratios.

rung 02
1 : 12

The galleria Vitrum ferreum

Material
iron trusses · glass roof · marble floor
What it carries
weather · daylight · a commercial register
Era
19c industrial

Iron replaces stone, glass replaces masonry. The arch survives the transition intact — the weight halves by an order of magnitude, and nobody misses anything.

rung 03
1 : 200

The market canopy Tegumentum lignum

Material
timber posts · corrugated metal sheet · hanging strings of produce
What it carries
rain · sun · the market's schedule
Era
timeless vernacular

No vault, no spring, no pretension. A covered pedestrian zone made by adding a flat plane above. The arcade's functional core, stripped of geometry.

rung 04
1 : 2,000

The fabric awning Tegumentum textile

Material
canvas on retractable aluminum frame
What it carries
sun · an implied roomline · the café's hours
Era
20c urban

Rain-shedding is given up. Sun and mood remain. The roof becomes seasonal, the roomline stays year-round.

rung 05
1 : 40,000

The string-light catenary Catenaria illuminata

Material
a dozen bulbs on a wire between two posts
What it carries
nothing physical · a convention of interior conditions
Era
21c hospitality

The bulbs define a room without enclosing one. Pedestrians adjust their pace on entering and leaving. The signal works fully unlit.

rung 06
1 : ∞

The dashed line Signum picturatum

Material
a few grams of thermoplastic paint
What it carries
the same roomline · now coded in a single convention
Era
administrative

The arcade has become a cartographic stroke. The physical substrate is gone; the agreement remains. The stage after this one is no object at all — a geofence, a polygon in a municipal planning layer.
